Resitex

Resitex is a high-performance, weather-resistant coating specifically designed for park homes. It provides a durable and long-lasting finish that protects against the elements, including rain, wind, and UV rays. With a variety of colors and finishes available, Resitex can be customized to match your personal style and preferences. Our team of experts ensures a flawless application, giving your park home a fresh, new look that will last for years to come.

Resitex Roofproof

Resitex Roofproof Services. Our Resitex Roofproof solutions are designed to provide long-lasting protection for your roof. This ensures that your roof remains dry and free from damage caused by trapped moisture. The flexible nature of Resitex Roofproof coatings allows them to expand and contract with temperature changes, preventing cracks and leaks. Our specialist team can apply these coatings to a variety of roof types, ensuring a perfect fit and finish. With Resitex Roofproof, you can enjoy peace of mind knowing that your roof is protected against the elements, reducing maintenance costs and extending the lifespan of your roof. Contact us today for a free consultation and quote.

Chassis Refurbishment

Ensure the longevity and safety of your park home with our professional chassis refurbishment services. Our experienced team specializes in restoring and reinforcing the chassis of your park home, providing a solid foundation for years to come. We meticulously inspect and treat any signs of wear, rust, or damage, using high-quality materials and techniques to ensure a durable and reliable refurbishment.

Our services include comprehensive inspection and assessment, rust removal and treatment, structural repairs and reinforcements, and applying protective coatings and finishes. We also offer regular maintenance and servicing to keep your chassis in top condition.

Kitchen Fitting

If you are considering a kitchen renovation we can arrange a fully bespoke design including electrics, tiling and plumbing. We can remove all of the old kitchen and install new electrics for any new appliances. We can strip back the entire kitchen to its shell, remove and environmentally dispose of all kitchen carcasses. We always aim to be tidy workers and keep areas clean of debris and trip hazards. Once all the walls are prepped we can install the kitchen, ready to paint and tile. We are experts at fitting carcasses and wall units to give your kitchen a high quality finish. In as little as 4 days we can fully fit out your new kitchen along with painting, tiling and flooring. I think you'll agree that this fully bespoke kitchen looks wonderful and the laminate vinyl flooring complements the kitchen perfectly!
